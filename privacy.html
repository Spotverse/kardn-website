Log Files

Kardn follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

User Data Deletion Requests

At Kardn, we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with control over your personal data. If you are a user of our app and would like to request the deletion of your data from our system, we offer a simple and straightforward process to do so.

Data Deletion Request Procedure:

  1. Submit a Deletion Request: You can submit a data deletion request by contacting our support team at info@kardn.co. Please provide the necessary information to verify your identity and account ownership to process your request efficiently.
  2. Verification Process: For security purposes, we may need to verify your identity and account ownership before proceeding with the deletion request. This is to ensure that only authorized individuals can request the deletion of personal data.
  3. Processing Time: Once we receive your data deletion request and verify your identity, we will initiate the data deletion process. Please note that it may take some time to complete the deletion, depending on the volume and complexity of the data involved.
  4. Confirmation of Deletion: Once your data has been successfully deleted, we will send you a confirmation email to notify you of the completion of the process.

Please be aware that certain data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes, such as compliance with legal obligations or resolving disputes. However, such retained data will be securely isolated and will not be used for any other purposes.

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we take every measure to protect your personal information and ensure transparency in our data processing practices. If you have any questions or concerns about our data deletion process or our privacy practices in general, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Kardn reserves the right to modify or update this data deletion request procedure as needed to comply with applicable laws and regulations. Any changes will be promptly reflected in this privacy policy.
